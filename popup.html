PixelOverlay

Status Off

When active, press Ctrl/⌘+V on a page to drop a clipboard image as a 50%-opacity overlay. Drag to move, drag the blue corner to scale (aspect ratio preserved). Sliders on the side panel control opacity (0–100%) and scale (0–2×); x/y/w/h are shown below them. State persists per URL across reloads.

Toggle buttons:

  • H — hide / show without losing position
  • L — lock position (clicks pass to page below)

Keyboard (overlay focused):

  • Esc — close and clear
  • H / L — hide / lock
  • ← → ↑ ↓ — nudge 1px (Shift = 10px)
  • Ctrl+↑/↓ — scale ±1% (Shift = 10%)
